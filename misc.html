yoga

JULY 2024

i've started to incorporate yoga into my daily routine in order to improve my hip mobility. my hips have always been tight and i've been struggling to sit on the ground for more than 90 mins. so far i'm enjoying the benefits and this is the most flexible i've ever been. the goal is to eventually be able to sit pain-free for those 2 hour long concerts.

pickleball

JULY 2024

recently i've also become hooked on the pickleball craze that has caught on over the past year. the sport is not too strenous on your body while still remaining competitive and providing me with a great source of exercise. i never expected to enjoy it as much as i have been.

tiktok

JULY 2024

i started posting violin content on tiktok for fun about a year ago. tiktok gave me a platform to be creative and branch outside of the classical music i've played up to this point. posting on tiktok would always be a "1 am activity" to me -- post whenever i was in the mood. this summer, i've been trying to post more often and trying to properly plan out my posts. this being said, i still want to ensure i remain true to my base, classical music, and that i don't get caught up with likes and views.
