I've kept this site to a fairly tight brief so far — experience, projects, a bit of
leadership work. That's useful for a first impression, but it doesn't leave much room
for the stuff that doesn't fit neatly into a bullet point: a half-formed idea about a
dataset, a note on something I built and what I'd do differently, or just what I've
been thinking about lately.

That's what this is for. No fixed schedule, no minimum word count — just notes as they
come up.

If you're here from the rest of the site: thanks for reading this far. More soon.
